Behavior

Esc used for: Minimize / Clear Text
Choose the action of pressing Esc: minimize the main-window, or clear the text of the current page.

Double-Click on TabPage: Rename / Switch Property / Delete
Choose the action of double-click on tabpage: Rename the page, or switch page property, or delete the current page.

Middle-Click on TabPage: Rename / Switch Property / Delete
Choose the action of middle-click on tabpage: Rename the page, or switch page property, or delete the current page.

Double-Click on Group: New Page / Level Up
Choose the action of double-click on group (i.e., on empty part of tabbar or page-list): New page, or go to the parent group.
